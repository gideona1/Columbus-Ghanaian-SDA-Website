Who we are

The Columbus Ghanaian Seventh-Day Adventist Church is a member congregation of the worldwide faith community of Christians known as Seventh-Day Adventists.

Purpose

Our primary purpose for existence is to prepare people for the imminent return of our Lord Jesus Christ.

John 14:1-3

Committment

We are, therefore, committed to "making disciples for Christ," and creating a church atmosphere in which we love one another, pray for one another, encourage one another, and help one another.

Loving God. Loving People.
